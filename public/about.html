About Me

Hi! I’m Ananyashree Kothari, a third-year Computer Science student at San Francisco State University with a passion for building impactful, user-centered software. I love turning ideas into real applications — from full-stack web platforms to interactive games and AI-driven projects that solve real-world problems. I’ve gained hands-on experience in Java, JavaScript, C++, HTML, CSS, Node.js, Express, and PostgreSQL, developing projects like Learn Loop, a skill-exchange platform that connects learners and mentors. These projects have strengthened my understanding of both front-end and back-end development and deepened my appreciation for creating smooth, intuitive user experiences. Outside of coding, I serve as the Vice President of the Indian Student Association, Secretary of the AI Agents Club, and Social Media Officer of Engineering Student's Advisory Board, where I organize events, foster collaboration, and encourage curiosity in technology and AI. I’m currently seeking software engineering internships where I can apply my skills, learn from experienced developers, and contribute to meaningful, scalable projects.
